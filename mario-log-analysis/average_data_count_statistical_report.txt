2.2 Kruskal-Wallis Test
   - Purpose: Compare three or more groups (non-parametric)
   - Null hypothesis: All groups have the same distribution
   - Alternative hypothesis: At least one group differs

2.3 Mann-Whitney U Test (Post-hoc)
   - Purpose: Pairwise comparisons after significant Kruskal-Wallis
   - Significance levels: *** p<0.001, ** p<0.01, * p<0.05, ns p≥0.05

2.4 Effect Size (r)
   - Purpose: Measure the magnitude of difference between groups
   - Interpretation:
     * r < 0.1: Small effect
     * r = 0.1-0.3: Medium effect
     * r = 0.3-0.5: Large effect
     * r > 0.5: Very Large effect
